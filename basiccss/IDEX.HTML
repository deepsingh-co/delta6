'KGF 3' UPDATE For all those eagerly waiting for an update on Yash's 'KGF 3', the wait ends here. IndiaToday.in has exclusively learnt that the film is all set to release in 2025. A source close to Hombale Films told us, “Yash-starrer ‘KGF 3’ is looking at mid-2025 or end of 2025 for the release date. The actor will begin shooting for the film at the end of next year. With this, Hombale Films is eyeing a stellar lineup for the upcoming years . They have booked Prabhas’ ‘Salaar’ for December-end of this year and are looking at going massively with ‘KGF 3’ in 2025.”
